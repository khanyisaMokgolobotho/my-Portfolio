About Me

I am Khanyisa Faith Mokgolobotho, a young Black South African and Junior Software Engineer with experience at Pycentric Pty Ltd and the CSIR NICIS SANReN team. My tech stack includes Python (FastAPI, Streamlit), Flutter, Firebase, MongoDB, Google Cloud, Angular, Java Spring Boot, PHP, and SQL. I am a 3x Hackathon winner, Chairperson of the Student Geekulcha Society, and former SRC Residence Officer. I believe in continuous learning and building meaningful solutions that drive real impact.
